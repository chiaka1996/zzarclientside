Our Work Process

Meeting & Listening

First, we will have a meeting and listen to your needs, what you want and how you want the event to be.

Planning

A team of Event Planners will be set up for the event. we will take your ideas and come up with a befitting plan for your Event.

Validation

Next phase is to get your validation. When all is cool, you will approve the Event plan, concept and budget.

Execution

At this satge, all you have to do is relax and watch us perform our magic. everything from logistic, costume and catering will be taken care of.

Feedback

After all the enjoyment and "Jollification", we always expect your Feedback, to enable us serve you better.
